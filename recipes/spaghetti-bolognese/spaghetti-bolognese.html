Spaghetti Bolognese

The classic spaghetti recipe for everyone to enjoy, not too hard to make while using an abundance of ingredients.

If you love italian food as much as I do, you know for sure this will satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • Beef Mince - 500g
  • Bacon Strips - 4 finely chopped
  • Spaghetti - 400g
  • Salt - 2 tsp
  • Pepper - 2 tsp
  • Olive oil - 1 tbs
  • Medium Onions - 2 finely chopped
  • Carrots - 2 trimmed and finely chopped
  • Celery sticks - 2 finely chopped
  • Garlic cloves - 2 finely chopped
  • Rosemary leaves - 3 picked and finely chopped

For the bolognese sauce

  • Plum Tomatoes - 2 400g cans
  • Basil leaves - 3/4 finely chopped
  • Dried Oregano - 1 tsp
  • Bay leaves - 2 fresh
  • Tomato Puree - 2 tbs
  • Beef stock cube - 1
  • Red Chilli - 1 seeds removed finely chopped (optional)
  • Red Wine - 125ml
  • Cherry Tomatoes - 6 sliced in half

For serving

  • Parmesan Cheese - 100g grated
  • Bread (optional)

Instructions

  1. Put a large saucepan on medium heat and add 1 tbs of Olive Oil
  2. Add 4 finely chopped bacon strips to the pan and fry until golden crisp
  3. Reduce the heat and add the onions, carrots, celery sticks, garlic cloves and the rosemary leaves all finely chopped, stir the vegetables often until soft.
  4. Increase the heat to medium-high, add the 500g of minced beef and cook while stirring for 4 mins until the meat turns brown.
  5. Add 2 cans of plum tomatoes and the finely chopped basil leaves, 2 tbs of tomato puree, 1 beef stock cube, 1 seedless and finely chopped red chilli, 125ml of Red Wine and 6 halves of cherry tomatoes, stir while breaking up the plum tomatoes.
  6. Bring to a boil then simmer and cover with a lid, cook for 1 hour while stirring occasionally until thickened.
  7. When the bolognese sauce is nearly finished, add to a pot 400g of spaghetti, 2 tsp of salt and pepper and 800ml of water.
  8. After 10 mins the spaghetti should be al dente, remove it and either add it to the saucepan with the bolognese sauce or set on a plate with the sauce on top.
  9. Add Parmesan Cheese on top or set on the side, as well as bread.
