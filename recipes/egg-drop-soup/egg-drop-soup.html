Egg-drop Soup

Rich in flavour and great for any hangover!

Egg-drop soup is the most easy to make soup. While it does lack complexity in the ingredients department, it is exactly that what makes it a great soup for beginners to try. It is also great at leaving you feeling full without upsetting your stomach with a lot of spices and condiments.

If you love eggs and mushrooms as much as I do, I bet your bottom dollar you will be making this every weekend for family and friends.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Stock - 3 cups
  • Salt - 1/4 tsp
  • Sugar - 1/4 tsp
  • Pepper - A pinch
  • Sesame Seed Oil - 1 tsp
  • Peas - 1/3 cup
  • Mushrooms - 1/3 cup
  • Cornstarch - 1 tbs
  • Water - 2 tbs
  • Spring Onions - 1/4 cup

Instructions

  1. In a pot add Chicken Stock and wait for it to boil.
  2. Next add the Salt, Sugar, Pepper and Sesame Seed Oil.
  3. Once the stock starts to boil add the vegetables.
  4. To thicken the soup, in a separate bowl add 1tbs of Cornstarch and 2tbs of water. Slowly add it to the soup till it reaches the right thickness.
  5. Next slowly add 1 slightly beaten egg then stir for 10 seconds.
  6. Serve in a bowl with green onions for decoration.
